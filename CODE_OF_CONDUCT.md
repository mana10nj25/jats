## 5. Enforcement  

### Reporting Violations  
If you observe or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:
1. Report it privately via email to **mana10nj20@gmail.com**
2. Include: a brief description, links/screenshots, and timestamp(s).  
3. Reports are treated confidentially by the project maintainers.

### Enforcement Actions  
Depending on severity, maintainers may:
- Issue a private or public warning.  
- Remove comments, issues, or PRs.  
- Temporarily or permanently ban offenders.  
- Escalate to GitHub or legal authorities if necessary.  

The enforcement team follows a **three-strike policy** for non-malicious but repeated violations.
